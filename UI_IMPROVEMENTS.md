## üí° Design Philosophy

**Modern, Professional, Engaging**

1. **Color Psychology**
   - Blue (professional, trustworthy)

   - Smooth transitions

   - High contrast ratios
   - Clear text readability
   - Intuitive navigation
   - Meaningful colors (not just decoration)
